About the Industry

Networking design, implementation, and maintenance is the industry of interest. This field focuses on building, protecting, and maintaining systems that allow devices to communicate reliably and securely.

Career of Interest - Network Architect

Network Architects are responsible for planning, implementing, securing, and maintaining internet infrastructure. They design networks to ensure efficiency, reliability, and security.

Ethical Issue

Networks can be intrusive and censorious. Because networks control access and data flow, they can be used to limit privacy or restrict communication.

Personal Values

My personal values push me to make systems as fair and transparent as possible. Consent should be required at all stages, and any violations of privacy or censorship must be justified. Mistakes should be addressed and restitution ensured.

Cybersecurity Issues

Even with strong security, systems can be forced to include backdoors by governments. These backdoors can be abused by both governments and malicious actors.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

Assign logical network components carefully and limit automatic configuration. Employee workstations should never directly communicate with core network layers.

Follow the principle of least privilege so users only have access to what they need. This prevents small breaches from becoming major issues.

Balance security and availability. Data must be protected but still accessible enough to be useful. Too much of either creates problems.
